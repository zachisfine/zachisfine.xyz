discord-auditor

security audits for discord servers

express 5 vue 3

screenshots

features

  • server permission audit with role & bot mapping
  • bot access review with per-bot risk scoring
  • flags administrator-level bots added by unknown members
  • history of past audit runs, kept locally
  • express 5 backend, vue 3 spa frontend
  • runs locally — no data leaves your machine

intended for

server owners and admins who inherited a server with dozens of bots, or ran one long enough that they can't remember who added the giveaway bot with administrator permissions. periodic security review without hand-scanning role tables.
