discord-spades

full spades card game inside discord

discord.js v14 node.js

screenshots

features

  • 4-player team and free-for-all modes
  • full spades rules including bidding & blind nil
  • games resume after bot restarts
  • 5-minute inactivity timeout with auto-fold
  • hand viewing locked during blind nil rounds
  • slash commands and select-menu ui
  • button ids stay valid across restarts

intended for

discord communities that want a proper card-game night without leaving their voice chat — friend groups, small tournaments, or servers with a games channel that isn't just chess and connect-four.
