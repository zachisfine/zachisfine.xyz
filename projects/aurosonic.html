aurosonic

discord music bot with a queue that survives restarts

discord.js v15-dev ffmpeg

screenshots

features

  • queue survives bot restarts
  • select-menu ui for browsing & controls
  • discord.js v15-dev with explicit ffmpeg piping
  • youtube, soundcloud, and direct-link sources
  • per-guild volume & playback settings
  • auto-resume of previous session on startup

intended for

self-hosters who want a music bot they actually control — no premium tier gating skip commands, no queue wiped by a random reboot, no ad breaks between tracks. built for one server or a small handful, not a public monetized fleet.
